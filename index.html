Estergebirge: Walchensee – Garmisch (Hohe Kisten, Krottenkopf, Hoher Fricken, Wank)

  • Speed Hiking
  • T6
  • Sat 18 Sep, 05:20

Let’s link up two of the most famous sights of the Bavarian Alps (Walchensee and the view of Zugspitze from Garmisch), along the Estergebirge. This will satisfy the needs of any peakbagger, with at least seven named summits.
