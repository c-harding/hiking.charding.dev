Amperau

  • ↔︎ 51.6 km
  • ↗︎ 209 m
  • Tue 23 Jul, 13:00

The roads to the north of Munich are nice and quiet, and pass through some lovely sleepy Bavarian villages. This route follows the course of the river Amper upstream, after crossing over the slight ridge from Neufahrn.
