Furgler & Hexenkopf vom See

  • Two Days
  • ↔︎ 36.9 km
  • ↗︎ 2843 m
  • T4
  • Sat 3 Aug, 14:00
  • 0/10 registered, 10 spaces available

This hike starts less than three hours from Munich, but climbs up to summits of 3004 m (Furgler) and 3035 m (Hexenkopf), higher than anything in Germany! While Furgler is one of the most popular mountains in Austria, Hexenkopf will be much quieter.
