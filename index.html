Wildalpjoch and Wendelstein from Brannenburg

  • ↔︎ 19.7 km
  • ↗︎ 1575 m
  • T3
  • Sun 23 Jun, 06:40

Wendelstein is one of the most recognisable peaks around, and it provides panoramic views over the fields of Bavaria to the north, and so much of the Alps to the south. Wildalpjoch is more wild, and should provide a more peaceful, tourist-free summit. Let's visit both on our journey into the Mangfallgebirge.

  • T4
  • Sun 14 Jul, 06:55
