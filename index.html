Stolzenberg and Roßkopf

  • ↔︎ 9.2 km
  • ↗︎ 630 m
  • T2
  • Sun 18 Oct, 09:00

This should be a lovely hike among the autumn leaves, around the Spitzingsee ski area. There are three summits without too much ascent/descent between them, and we should get good views south towards Schinder and Sonnwendjoch in the Mangfallgebirge.
